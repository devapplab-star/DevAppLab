PillPlanner

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

At PillPlanner, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Your Privacy First: We designed PillPlanner with privacy in mind. All your medication data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your personal health information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Local Device Data: Medication names, dosages, schedules, and reminder preferences stored on your device only
  • App Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about how you use the app to improve user experience
  • Device Information: Basic device type and OS version for app optimization

3. How We Use Your Information

Information we collect is used for:

  • Providing medication reminders and tracking functionality
  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Sending push notifications for medication reminders (only with your permission)
  • Analyzing app usage patterns to enhance features

4. Data Storage and Security

On-Device Storage: All your medication and health-related data is stored exclusively on your device using secure local storage mechanisms.

No Cloud Sync: We do not currently offer cloud synchronization. This means your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

Security Measures: We implement reasonable security measures to protect the information stored locally on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

PillPlanner may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics (anonymous, aggregated usage data)
  • Crash reporting to fix app issues

These services are bound by their own privacy policies and are used only to improve app quality.

6. Push Notifications

We may send push notifications to remind you about your medication. You can enable or disable notifications at any time through your device settings or within the app preferences.

7. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your stored medication data within the app
  • Delete: Delete your data by uninstalling the app or using the in-app delete feature
  • Export: Export your data for personal backup (where supported)

8. Children's Privacy

PillPlanner is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy and, in some cases, we may provide additional notice through the app.
